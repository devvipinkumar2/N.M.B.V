About Us

Niwasi Mook Badhir Vidyalaya ,Parli Vaijnath is dedicated to providing quality education and care for physically impaired students from Grade 1 through 7. Our school focuses on inclusive learning, personal development, and skill-building in a supportive and compassionate environment. We believe every child deserves dignity, opportunity, and the confidence to build a bright and independent future.
